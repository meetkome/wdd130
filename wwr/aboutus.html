Meet Our Rafting Guides

Clam the Slam

Cam is one of our newest river guides here at Roaring Rapids. He has given his riders the experience of a lifetime.
We have never met a customer who was disappointed after a rafting trip with Cam as a guide. Cam is full of fun, adventure, and of course, a love for safety. You will have the adventure of a lifetime when you sign up to ride with Cam through the beautiful rivers of Moab Canyon

Nate the Great

Nate has been with Roaring Rapids since the beginning. Right out of college, he traveled the world in search of the best rivers to ride. He is one of the most adventurous and risk taking guides...
but don’t worry, you will feel safe in his hands. He sticks to the safety rules and makes sure that everyone has the best experience possible. Sign up to ride with Nate in one of our Grand Canyon rides.

Brit the Fit

Britt is such a fun guide. She loves to do anything related to the one of her greatest passions, the outdoors. Britt will help you appreciate nature with her great knowledge of the areas she guides and great fun with her sense of humor. .She is one of our best Family experts. Britt will take you through the most amazing rivers of the Yellowstone National Park. If you want a great ride, hop on Britt’s crew

We are Waiting to Hear From You

Sister A’s Roaring Rapids was founded in 1998 in Northern California with 2 rafts and 2 tour guides. It started as a small family operation and has now expanded to a staff of 16 tour guides, 25 rafts and spans 5 states. With a love for the great outdoors and Mother Nature’s power, we have rapids and other nature tours 52 weeks of the year. It’s always rapids time, somewhere. We invite you to look at what we have to offer and take a chance on the greatest experience you will ever have... Come and join us by Roaring on the Rapids with us soon.
